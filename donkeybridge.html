Donkey Bridge

A vocabulary app for visual learners

Introduction

This is a case study about ux design concept created in spring 2020.

My Role:

UI/UX Designer

Challenge & Goal

Challenge

How might we help visual learners to memorise new vocabulary quickly on the go.

Goal

Creating a vocabulary learning app which supports the easy memorising of vocabulary in a very visual way.

Methods & Tools

Methods

  • Competitor Research
  • User Research (User Interviews)
  • User Persona
  • Task Analysis
  • Crazy 8's Technique
  • Low Fidelity Prototyping
  • User Testing
  • High Fidelity Wireframes

Tools

  • Adobe Comp App (Ipad)
  • Marvel App
  • Whimsical
  • Pen&Paper&Postits
  • Voice Recorder App
  • Videocamera App

Research

The research was splitted in a competitor research and a user research phase.The competitor research gave me a better understanding of what functionalities of vocabulary learning apps are currently existing. The user research gave me insights of the needs and goals of my target group.
