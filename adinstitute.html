Art and Design Institute

An platform that connects experts with their students online.

Overview

This is a case study about an ux design concept created in 2020.

My role: UX-Designer

Challenge

Artists and Designers need a way to share their skills online, which can lead to scalable income.

Goal

Creating a responsive webapp where creative experts could share their skills remotely and meet their students online.

Process Steps

  • Competitor Research
  • User Research (User Interviews)
  • User Persona
  • Task Analysis
  • User Flow
  • User Journey
  • Low Fidelity Prototyping
  • Mid Fidelity Prototyping
  • High Fidelity Wireframes
  • User Testing
